About Our Logistics Operations

EMA Kiarago Transport & Logistics Limited is a Nairobi-based logistics company specializing in customs clearance, cargo handling, and cross-border transport. We provide professional solutions for import, export, and transit cargo.

Our operations include clearance through KRA ICMS, IDF processing, EX1 transit entries, and coordination with port health and border authorities to ensure full compliance and timely delivery.
