About Us

EMA Kiarago Transport & Logistics Limited is located along Lunga Lunga Road, next to the House of Maji and Shell Petrol Station.

We provide professional Clearing and Forwarding services designed to ensure your cargo moves smoothly, safely, and on time. We handle all customs procedures, documentation, and logistics coordination so you can focus on your business while we take care of the rest.

With in-depth knowledge of Kenyan customs regulations and regional trade requirements, we guarantee fast clearance, reduced delays, and full compliance.
